04 — The Experience
Your journey,
phase by phase.

Every moment is meticulously choreographed — from your first briefing to the moment you touch down, forever changed.

Phase 01 — Preparation
Selection & Training
A 6-month program at our Mojave facility. Medical screening, centrifuge training, zero-gravity simulation, and mission familiarization — tailored to your physical profile.
Phase 02 — Ascent
Launch & Orbital Insertion
Depart from Nexus Spaceport at dawn. Experience 3.2G during ascent, then sudden weightlessness as you cross the Kármán line. First view of Earth's curvature within 8 minutes.
Phase 03 — Orbit
The Voyage
Your orbital experience begins. Michelin-starred zero-gravity dining, guided stargazing sessions, Earth observation labs, and unstructured time in the panoramic lounge.
Phase 04 — Return
Re-Entry & Homecoming
Controlled re-entry with plasma trail visible through rear cameras. Parachute-assisted ocean landing. Helicopter transfer to a private ceremony celebrating your achievement.
